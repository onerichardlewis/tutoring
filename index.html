About Me

Hello and welcome to my website. I am a local Maths tutor based in Handbridge covering Chester and the surrounding areas.

During school I loved studying Maths and Physics and can wholeheartedly say this was due to the teachers’ passion for the subjects. I hope to offer the same enthusiasm through tutoring, holding a Bachelor's Degree in Physics (2:1).

I like to get to know my students and also view the topics from their different perspectives in order to get the best results.

Tuition is available from KS3 to A-level standard and the first lesson is free in order for you to ensure that I am a suitable tutor for your son or daughter. Subsequent lessons are £30 per hour for a qualified, DBS checked, outgoing individual.

I look forward to hearing from all prospective students and parents in the near future.
